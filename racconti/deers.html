High Council of the Pretty Deers

The Ancient Doctrine of the Seven Deers

In the ethereal epoch of ages bygone, when the celestial bodies danced in harmonious cadence across the cosmic tapestry, a conclave of venerable Deers convened beneath the veil of a resplendent lunar glow. Gathered in solemn unity, these wise custodians of ancient wisdom sought to inscribe the sacred tenets that would govern the delicate dance of hearts entwined in this magnum opus, "The Ancient Doctrine of the Seven Deers."

In that sacred assembly, where the whispers of time and eternity merged, the luminaries of erudition embarked upon the most consecrated endeavor and transcribed the alchemy of emotions into immutable laws, weaving a tapestry of eternal truths that would guide the romantic pilgrims through the labyrinthine passages of devotion.

Thus, from their efforts under the celestial contemplation emerged this book, "The Ancient Doctrine of the Seven Deers", encapsulating the sacred covenant between souls, echoing through the ages and whispering its divine counsel to those who dare to seek the sublime alchemy of love across the aeons.

 

Sacred Laws

1. The Victorious Wolf must love his Pretty Deer.

2. The Victorious Wolf must love no other woman but his Pretty Deer.

3. The Victorious Wolf is subject to the Ancient Doctrine of the Seven Deers, as well as the Ancient Doctrine of the Victorious Wolves. In case of conficts, refer to Law 4.

4. The Ancient Doctrine of the Seven Deers always takes precedence over the Ancient Doctrine of the Victorious Wolves.

5. The Victorious Wolf should not make trailers, in the sense of starting to say one thing and then not finishing it and saying he will say it in the future.

6. The Victorious Wolf and the Pretty Deer must say "I love you" regularly. Any language accepted.

7. The Victorious Wolf and the Pretty Deer must support each other's dreams and encourage personal growth.

8. The Victorious Wolf and the Pretty Deer must dance together.

9. The Victorious Wolf and the Pretty Deer must roast each other often.

10. The Pretty Deer must participate in the expenses when she is with her Victorious Wolf.

11. The Victorious Wolf must protect his Pretty Deer from everything and everyone.

12. The Victorious Wolf must not lie to his Pretty Deer even with white lies.

13. The Pretty Deer must call her Victorious Wolf in many cute ways, such as فؤاد, سعدون, ذئب.

14. If the Victorious Wolf is hungry (of love), he must kill and eat his Deer with love (meaning: strangling her in hugs).

15. It is forbidden for the Pretty Deer to decide what the Victorious Wolf will eat before she knows his food tastes well.

16. The Pretty Deer can dress in the prettiest ways inside and even outside the house/hotel.

17. Well, of course, the Pretty Deer must love no other man but her Victorious Wolf.

18. Perhaps and Maybe are perfectly acceptable answers to the question "Would you marry me?" and the like (e.g. "Do you marry me?", "Do you want to marry me?", etc.). The same goes for exclamations and more of the same kind.

19. The Victorious Wolf must wake up early the Pretty Deer if she is still sleeping.
